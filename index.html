Magic Number

Instructions

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win by matching your score with the Magic Number. You loose if its not exact.
  • The value can only be found when clicked on.
  • Each time the game starts the crystal values change.
